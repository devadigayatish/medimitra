Who We Are

Medimitra is a forum created by Pune Doctor Patient Forum (PDPF), a non-profitable registered society, to exchange information about ethical & reliable doctors and patients feedback.

Our Mission

We are all aware of the growing ‘trust deficit’ between patients and doctors.Pune-Doctor-Patient-Forum seeks to bring together sane voices from within the community of doctors and patients to begin to overcome this situation and to foster rational, ethical health care.

Not only patients but many doctors who want to do rational and ethical practice are suffering due to gross commercialization and consumerism which have led to irrational investigations, procedures and surgeries, kickbacks for referrals. As a result there is mutual loss of faith between doctors and patients.

To overcome this situation would require a whole series of policy-measures to move towards a system wherein private-practitioners would be paid from public fund on the principle of ‘standard payment for standard care’ and hence doctor-patient relations would not based on the market-logic. Many of the doctors and citizen are aware that such system exists in some European countries, even in some third world countries and in Canada. This system is part of a national system called Universal Health Care (UHC). Yes, it’s a long way and quite an uphill task to bring UHC in India.

However, even today some enlightened citizens and doctors can come together to foster relations based not on commercial logic but primarily geared to the spirit of giving and eliciting ethical professional health care. The Forum seeks to achieve this immediate objective and at the same time would try to contribute to the above mentioned long-term solution.

Towards this end the Forum would try to initiate and foster different activities like –

  • To build a network of enlightened citizens and doctors trying to do rational, ethical practice and to prepare a data base of such doctors so as to offer a list of the doctors to a patient in need.
  • To organize discussions among such doctors and citizens (on topics like Patients’ Rights and Responsibilities) to understand each other’s problems and to help each other to take remedial steps
  • To prepare and publish with the help of such doctors,standard educational material in lay language.This may be in the form of Frequently Asked Questions and their answers about scientific treatment of various health-problems ranging from medical problems like diabetes, high blood pressure etc. to problems like hernia, piles, appendicitis etc. etc. which require surgical intervention.
  • To create a web-site for wider dissemination of these activities and to upload this educational material so that on the one hand a wider community of citizens can benefit and on the other hand a wider community of like-minded doctors can endorse such material and also give suggestions for improvements.
  • To create a web based platform where patients will give opinion about doctors in a structured manner.
  • To foster medical diagnostic and treatment centers as well as good quality generic stores which demonstrate that modern, ethical, humane rational health care is possible at much lower cost in a sustainable manner than what is available commercially
  • To liaison with other such groups in India and abroad
  • To try to advocate for policy-measures which will undermine corporatization, commercialization of health care and promote, rational, ethical health care
  • To contribute to the movement for ‘Health and Health care for All’

The Forum will work in a democratic transparent manner and will be open to all who agree with its perspective and want to contribute to its work. The Forum is formed by volunteer-citizens from various professional and social backgrounds and like-minded doctors. It is expected that the doctors associated with the Forum would follow scientific diagnostic and treatment guidelines; would avoid unnecessary expenses for patients; would give sufficient time to patients, especially during the first consultation; would be willing to answer/arrange to answer reasonable queries and doubts by patients; and would have transparency regarding fees and charges. Forum believes that patients too have certain responsibilities.

From the desk

Dear friends,

We all know that though there is a near collapse of the trust between doctors and patients to a large extent; not everything is lost.

There is a segment of practicing doctors around us who are honest, ethical and rational. We as a society have a responsibility to nurture and support these conscientious doctors.

‘Pune Doctor Patient Forum’ is an outcome of brainstorming among doctors as well as citizen for way ahead to bridge the gap between conscientious doctors and needy patients. The forum consists of stalwarts from the medical and social fields. Please refer to our advisors.


I and many of my doctor friends get regular queries from the concerned patients regarding suitable – ethical, rational medical professional in City of Pune and we have been referring them to the medical professional around whom we believe ethical and rational. Taking this process of referral to non-personal level; and for the benefit of many; ‘Pune Doctor Patient Forum’ intends to start a counseling service to needy patients that would offer the choice of the data base of ethical and rational doctor to the best of our knowledge, and as referred by citizen and doctors. We will not charge the patients nor receive any payment from doctors for this counseling service.

It is expected that the doctors associated with the Forum would follow scientific diagnostic and treatment guidelines; would avoid unnecessary expenses for patients; would give sufficient time to patients, especially during the first consultation; would be willing to answer/arrange to answer reasonable queries and doubts by patients; and would have transparency regarding fees and charges. Forum believes that patients too have certain responsibilities.

We have many more activities in mind; please refer to our brochure at this website.

We will be posting dates of the general meetings regularly on this website. (Now under development) Please join us in large numbers – both doctors and citizens – let us start a movement towards ethical, rational healthcare in our city.

With warm regards,
Yours truly,
Dr. Arun Gadre.
